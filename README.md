# MKA Trading Services

Company profile website for MKA Trading Services FZ-LLC. The site presents the company overview, services, industries served, and contact details, with a modern responsive layout, gallery, and light/dark theme toggle.

## Contents
- Landing and company profile sections
- Scope of activities and industries served
- Service gallery with hover effects
- Contact details and company information
- Light/dark theme toggle with persisted preference

## How to view
Open index.html in a browser.
